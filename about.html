Brief Biography

Miguel is a dedicated public servant and writer based out of Brookyln, NY. Miguel has a background in municipal government service, non-profit social work, and federal law enforcement. He enjoys exploring thoughtful works of fiction, learning about the unfamiliar, and being challenged to the point of debilitating frustration by games and puzzles.

Please join (and encourage) him on his journey through dev bootcamp!
